dpdk 接口 pcie bar 无法 mmap 映射的问题

函数为 pci_map_resource().rte_mem_map().mem_map().mmap()

查看 dmesg ，发现如下报错，

[Thu Jul 14 15:14:19 2022] Lockdown: vpp: raw io port access is restricted; see man kernel_lockdown.7
[Thu Jul 14 15:14:20 2022] igb_uio 0000:06:00.0: uio device registered with irq 42
[Thu Jul 14 15:14:20 2022] Lockdown: vpp: direct PCI access is restricted; see man kernel_lockdown.7
[Thu Jul 14 15:14:20 2022] igb_uio 0000:0a:00.0: uio device registered with irq 42
[Thu Jul 14 15:14:20 2022] Lockdown: vpp: direct PCI access is restricted; see man kernel_lockdown.7

man kernel_lockdown 发现， 由 EFI Secure Boot mode 开启的 kernel_lockdown feature 导致，

The use of direct PCI BAR access.

需要关闭 EFI Secure Boot mode ，

随后就可以正常接管了，
